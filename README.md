# Moreflix
In December 2023 I was meeting a girl that I really liked and we always recommended movies and series to watch, and one day I told her to watch some of these online, when searching I see that all these platforms are paid and the ones that existed were free They did not provide the best service or reliability, so thanks to that situation, Moreflix emerged, so that other people do not have restrictions on both payment and a good user experience.
